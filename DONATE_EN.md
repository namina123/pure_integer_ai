# Support this research

First of all, **whether or not you support it — thank you for reading this far.**

This research explores "an intelligent system built on a pure-integer substrate" — a personal project with no institutional affiliation and no commercial sponsorship. It does not chase short-term productization, nor does it compete for attention. It simply tries, quietly, to think one question through: **can integers carry learning and cognition?**

All code and the paper are released under permissive licenses; anyone may read, use, and verify them free of charge. The research itself will not stop for lack of funding.

If any of the following resonates with you, you might consider supporting:

- the exploration makes you think "this is interesting; I'd like it to continue";
- you want independent research to have a little more breathing room;
- you'd like to cast a small vote of confidence for "things without immediate ROI."
